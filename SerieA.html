Serie A Future Stars (20/21)

Alessandro Bastoni (22 Inter Milan Center Back)

Bastoni has played 33 of 38 matches in the Serie A assiting twice. Bastoni wins 0.7 tackles, 1.5 arial duels, and 2.2 clearences per 90 minutes in the Serie A this season. He is set to play in the 2020 Euros for Italy. Inter Milan won the Italian League comfortably with many players stepping up but Bastoni played a major part in their title win. They comfortably defeated Italy giants Juventus. Bastoni had Ronaldo locked down resulting in a 2-0 win for Inter Milan.

Gianluigi Donnarumma (22 Milan Goalkeeper)

Donnarumma has been playing for Milan's first team debut in the Serie A at the age of 16 years and 242 days. He has been the starting goalkeeper for Milan since 2016. At the age of 22 he can be considered a veteran. Donnarumma has 14 clean sheets this season with a Milan team that placed second in the League. Donnarumma started his senior international career at the age of 17 years and six months. He has plenty of years ahead of him yet he already has the knowledge of a veteran. He will be a presence in-front of goal for many years to come.

Dejan Kulusevski (21 Juventus F.C. Winger/Midfielder)

Kulusevski has played 35 of 38 matches in the Serie A scoring scoring 3 and assisting 4. Kulusevski is apart of a Juventus team that has struggled this season but he has been the bright light in an otherwise dead season for Juventus. Kulusevski has saved Juventus many crucial points throughout the season. He mainly comes of the bench later on in the game if the team is struggling. Even though Juventus struggled in the League, they managed to win the Coppa Italia this season. Kulusevski scored 3 goals in 5 matches in the Coppa Italia, proving to be an important player in their cup win.
